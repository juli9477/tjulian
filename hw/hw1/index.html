Interactive Design is field of study that focuses on user experience. It centers around the communication of media through cooperative processes between people and technology. Interactive design aims in creating simple, defined target and clear direction and understandable screen interface. Interactive Design contains six main elements, user control, responsiveness, real-time interactions, connectedness, personalization and playfulness. Many times people confuse Interactive Design with Interaction. Interactive Design includes the building of a meaningful uses of hardware and systems, while Interaction Design is the design of those hardware and systems. Both of these designs are essential to each other.
